Hi, I'm Abhishek Vishwanath

I’m a passion driven Software Engineer and Automation enthusiast. I love building cost-effective software solutions that empower users to efficiently perform at their roles and add value to their lives.

After graduating with a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication, I started my career in Information Technology industry with Cognizant Technology Solutions where I had an amazing opportunity to collaborate with brilliant developers and build highly scalable complex web applications. My analytical thinking and strong programming competency have left me with highly satisfied clients and Spot Award. My desire to explore more in Web Technology and Cloud Computing related domains persuaded me to pursue master’s in Information Systems from Northeastern University.

During my six months Co-op at Boehringer Ingelheim, I had a unique opportunity to learn Robotic Process Automation and deliver an end-to-end automation solution that saved the company 2000 hours/year.

My diverse experience in the technology industry and strong academic foundation in computer science have cast me to be a competent software developer capable of delivering end-to-end product solution. However, I consider myself a ‘forever student’, eagerly learning new technology by pushing myself out of my comfort zone.

Aside from my academics, I enjoy traveling and exploring new places, attend virtual tech conferences, participate in Hackathons and, watch movies and sitcoms from a wide array of genres.
